SHIPPING INFORMATION

Arrohome is pleased to offer flat rate shipping to all 50 US States.

Your order will be shipped to you at a flat rate of USD$20.00.

Once you have ordered your product online, we know you will want it in a hurry so we've teamed up with Australia Post to offer next day dispatch for all orders.

How long will it take for me to get my order and how can I track it?


Once your order is packed and dispatched, we will send you an email with a unique tracking number, this will allow you to track your order. You can use the USPS or Australia Post website to track your parcel. From dispatch date it should take 6-10 business days to arrive.

Will I have to pay tax and duties on my order?


Your order may incur local duties and taxes. Duties and taxes are determined by the customs agency within your country. Payments of any duties and taxes is in the responsibility of the recipient, and these fees are collected at the time of delivery.
