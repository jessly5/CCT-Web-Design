WELCOME VISITORS


On behalf of Square One, it is my pleasure to extend a warm welcome to all our international and out-of-town visitors. Whether you are already here on vacation or planning a trip to the Greater Toronto Area, we invite you to include Square One in your travel plans.

For those of you who are Travel Professionals organizing a shopping excursion or tour itinerary, please contact me personally and we can work together to customize your group program.

We look forward to welcoming you to Square One.

Sincerely,


Lesley Boughen
